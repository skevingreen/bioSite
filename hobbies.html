    Kelli's Hobbies

    Kelli has enjoyed arts and crafts since a young age. Some examples of what they enjoyed working on include embroidery, painting ceramics, and decorating clothing with puff paint. That love of crafting has only grown stronger over the years, and Kelli is constantly trying new things. Her current favorite hobbies include quilting, journaling, and making paper flowers.

For example, old maps, postcards, damaged books, etc. can all be repurposed. Kelli is also a member of several craft groups that exchange crafts with one another on a regular basis. These groups offer up a fun way to share time and ideas with like-minded individuals.
